Alien Healthcare is a student-led startup redefining pain management through wearable technology.

Founded by Jinay Gala (B.Tech, Somaiya Vidyavihar University), the venture began as an engineering project.

Pre-Incubated at RIIDL, we combine embedded systems and design thinking to create safe, portable, and drug-free recovery solutions.

Embedded Systems
Design Thinking

The Problem

Safety Concerns

Lack of precision leads to guesswork. High risks of burns or frostbite for seniors or sensitive skin.

Inefficient & Inconsistent

Gel packs lose temperature within 15–20 minutes. Heating pads require a power source, limiting mobility.

Limited Mobility

No reliable, portable solution enables consistent therapy in real-world conditions.

Fragmented UX

Users forced to choose between effectiveness, safety, or convenience. No tool integrates all three.

The Solution

Introducing
ThermoEase

Active Therapy,
Zero Dependencies.

Eliminates the need for freezers or microwaves. Our technology actively generates both heat and cold in a single wearable band.
