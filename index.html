THE PROBLEM

Inefficient & Inconsistent

Gel packs lose temperature within 15–20 minutes. Heating pads require a power source, limiting mobility.

Safety Concerns

Lack of precision leads to guesswork. High risks of burns or frostbite for seniors or sensitive skin.

Limited Mobility

No reliable, portable solution enables consistent therapy in real-world conditions.

Fragmented UX

Users forced to choose between effectiveness, safety, or convenience. No tool integrates all three.
